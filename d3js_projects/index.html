Middle class health check

A base for journalists to keep up to date with the status of the middle class in America. This website presents updated charts of the most important indicators of how the situation of the middle class.

Real GDP

The most important measure of the economy. This effects everybody, including the middle class.

Change in GDP

A different way to measure the GDP.

Median Income

The median income of an average American is a strong indicator of what is happening to the wages of the middle class.

Employment

Employment figures are an important indicator to judge job security and the direction of wages.

Debt to disposable income

A measure of how much disposable income American have..

Inflation

A critical measure of the value of money. The difference between inflation and wages is an indicator of what is happening to the wages of the middle class.
